Online, Underground is an in-progress written history of the first social media revolution, chronicling the culture and technology of online messageboards, from the dawn of computer networking to the present.

"I honestly go in expecting the worst from a lot of dry, academic studies but this one is unusually genuine and heartfelt." - Jeff Atwood, co-founder of Stack Overflow and Discourse
